Our Foundation

Grevia Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a dynamic startup driven by a young and passionate team backed with decades of cumulative experience across diverse industries.

The company operates in the domains of customised heat exchangers, CNC & automation solutions, and sustainable technologies aimed at reducing carbon emissions, lubricants & specialty chemicals.

Its innovative offerings find applications across food, pharmaceutical, chemical, plywood, paper, automotive, and general manufacturing sectors, with a strong focus on delivering value while supporting industries in their journey towards sustainability and carbon footprint reduction.

Technical Expertise & Ties

  • Heat Exchangers: Products designed and certified to national and international codes through collaborations with certifying bodies.
  • R&D: A dedicated R&D centre at Taloja for the development of lubricants and specialty chemicals to meet evolving industrial requirements.
  • CNC & Automation: Deployment of advanced high-end CNCs and special-purpose machines (SPMs) integrated with AI-driven automation.
  • Carbon Emissions: Technical tie-ups with accredited agencies for carbon emission study and evaluation.

Global Presence

Grevia Solutions is actively exploring international markets to extend its innovative solutions to customers worldwide.

With a strong emphasis on technology, quality, and sustainability, the company aims to position itself as a trusted partner for industries in Southeast Asia, Middle East, Europe, African Sub-continent, and Australia.
